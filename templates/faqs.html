FAQs

Please check back closer to the date of the wedding for the most updated information. If you have any questions outside of what we have listed here please do not hesitate to reach out to us!

QUESTION
How do I RSVP?
ANSWER
Our invitations were sent via email only. Please click the link in the email to RSVP. If something isn't right, please reach out!
QUESTION
When should I RSVP by?
ANSWER
We ask that you RSVP by July 31st 2023.
QUESTION
Am I able to bring a guest?
ANSWER
When you RSVP you will see who is included under your party.
Any options, such as dietary restrictions or +1s, can be provided there.
QUESTION
When should I arrive?
ANSWER
We recommend arriving at least 24 hours before the wedding to get settled. Please keep in mind that Mexico is celebrating the day of the dead on November 2nd and it might be great to experience it.
QUESTION
Where should I stay?
ANSWER
We recommend looking for hotels within "Zona Romantica" (Romantic Zone) or around Zona Centro. These are walkable areas near most of the city's attractions.
QUESTION
Should I buy a SIM card?
ANSWER
We recommend purchasing a sim card once you arrive in Mexico. You can get a chip with plan "Amigo SL200" for 200 pesos with 3gbs of data and unlimited calling. These chips can be bought in OXXOs (a popular convenience store in Mexico). If not you can contact your provider to ask about long distance/roaming options.
QUESTION
Should I rent a car?
ANSWER
We recommend taking taxis, ride share, or walking around the city.
QUESTION
Is there parking available at the venue?
ANSWER
Yes, there is limited parking at the venue. But PLEASE DO NOT DRINK AND DRIVE.
QUESTION
What will the weather be like?
ANSWER
The weather can be relatively hot during the day (20-30°C) and cooler in the evening (18-20°C). We recommend light and breathable fabrics. We will be holding the ceremony outdoors (weather permitting).
QUESTION
Do you have a gift registry?
ANSWER
Your presence on our special day is the greatest gift we could ask for. We appreciate the effort involved in joining us for our wedding, so please, no gifts!
