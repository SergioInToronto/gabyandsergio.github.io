Puerto Vallarta International Airport (PVR)
FLIGHT
The city's airport is named Licenciado Gustavo Díaz Ordaz International Airport (PVR). This is the best way to visit Puerto Vallarta.
View
Transportation
TAXI
Taxi is the best way to get to/from the airport.

Within the city walking, taxi, and ride share (e.g. Uber) are all good options.
