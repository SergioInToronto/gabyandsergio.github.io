Travel

The closest major city to the villa is Verona. We recommend flying to Verona Villafranca Airport (VRN) or Venice Marco Polo (VCE) depending on your plans.

Venice Marco Polo (VCE)
FLIGHT
VCE is a larger airport than VRN so you may find cheaper/direct flights. However, the airport is 1.5 hrs from Verona by train.
